How Digital Radionics Works

This is the typical workflow of the AetherOnePy:

Case(s)

A target, which could be a person, an animal, a place, an abstract energy field or something you want to analyze and energetically balance.

You begin with typing the name of your case object / subject and then maybe enter more details, a small description, some notes, etc.

Session(s)

A session is when you sit down and analyze, broadcast and make notes.

Analysis

For an analysis you select a list of so called "rates" (or just names of energetic signatures) and perform the analysis.
